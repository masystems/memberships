Cloud-Lines Memberships

Manage your organisations memberships

Already a member?

Simple Member sign-up

Cloud-Lines Memberships makes the process of joining your membership organsation as simple as possible. Once prospective members have signed-up to CLM, their dashboard enables them to join any organisation using CLM with just 2 clicks!

Subscription-based card payments

Still spending hours chasing payments throughout the year? CLM uses the subscription payment model to setup recurring annual or monthly payments. This means chasing members for payments can be a thing of the past! What will you do with all that free time?!

Record Offline payments

It's a fact of modern life that payment systems need to accomodate both offline payments and electronic card payments. CLM provides the functionality to record Cheque, Standing-Order, Paypal, or any other external or "offline" payment type.

Payment reminders

CLM provide Admins a clear view of those members who have overdue payments (usually offline payers). A simple 2-click function enables the admin to send customised payment reminders to overdue members, logging the reminder in an audit trail.

Invoicing & receipts

Full integration with Stripe (card payments), and a whole bunch of receipt & invoice templates for offline-payers, means invoicing & receipts within CLM are a breeze.

Donations

If your organisation is able to accept donations, CLM not only records those payments, but also gives you a set of handy HTML buttons for your website or social-media presence - making it as easy as possible for people to donate.

Cloud-Lines-Memberships is an easy-to-use membership management system which offers a more streamlined way to manage members, simplify new-member sign-up, and optionally integrate with an existing Cloud-Lines.com package.

Cloud-Lines-Memberships functions as a stand-alone membership management system for any type of membership organisation, but is especially tuned to the Livestock & Animal Breeding sectors. Breed Societies, Oversight Organisations, NGOs, and Charities within those sectors will find CLM an intuitive system, tuned to their day-to-day requirements.

The optional integration with an existing Cloud-Lines.com package (pedigrees) ensures that new members are given access to your breeding system as soon as they're signed-up & paid. This integration also ensure that lapsed or removed members have their Cloud-Lines.com access similarly removed.
